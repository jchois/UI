WELCOME TO GROW

Started in 2008, Grow a small charity working with communities to create community gardens in their area. It's as much about growing community as it is about growing our food and flowers!

We have two gardens at present; one in the Waterworks Park in North Belfast and one in Helens Bay on Helen's Bay Organic Farm

GET INVOLVED

  1. Get involved in a community garden
  2. Join community events in the garden
  3. Cooking and healthy eating workshops
  4. Action days
